Who We Are

Compassionate Dental & General Healthcare

At Hanu Dental and Healthcare, Dr. Subrat Agnihotri leads a dedicated team committed to providing exceptional dental and general health services to the people of Shahabad and surrounding areas.

We combine modern technology with a gentle touch to deliver pain-free, effective treatments for patients of all ages. From routine check-ups to complex dental restorations, we've got you covered.

  • State-of-the-art digital X-ray & equipment
  • Qualified & experienced medical team
  • Hygienic, sterilized treatment environment
  • Affordable pricing with EMI options
  • Emergency dental care available
